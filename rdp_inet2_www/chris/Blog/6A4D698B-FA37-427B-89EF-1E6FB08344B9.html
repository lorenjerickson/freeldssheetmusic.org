Fabulous French Bread
 
Combine and mix to form batter:
        2 1/4 c warm water
        2 T sugar                      
        2 T oil
        1 T salt                          
        2 1/2 c flour
 
Add: 2 T dry yeast, SAF
        1 egg white
 
Add remaining flour:
        2 1/2 to 3 1/2 c flour
 
May need more flour depending on size of egg. Stir until the dough follows the spoon around. It should be slightly sticky to the touch.
 
Stir dough again every 5 minutes for 1/2 hour.
 
Divide the dough into two parts. Form dinner rolls, two large rolls/loaves, OR two logs.
 
Place on large cookie sheet that has been generously sprinkled with cornmeal.  Allow to rise 10-20 minutes.
 
Brush with beaten egg and bake at 400º for 30 minutes.
 
 
This is a quick, easy bread--from start to finish, 70 minutes.  Try something hot and fresh for your dinner table!
